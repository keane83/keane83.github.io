FAQ

Q: The game won’t start. What do I need to do first?
Zombie AR Shooter requires screen mirroring to a TV. Please use AirPlay (iPhone Control Center → Screen Mirroring) or connect via HDMI if supported.

Q: I can mirror to the TV, but detection fails or feels inaccurate.
For best results:
• Hold your phone as close as possible to the center of the TV screen when tapping Detect.
• After tapping Detect, keep your phone still for about 6 seconds.
• If prompted, adjust the on-screen rectangle on your phone so it matches the TV screen as closely as possible.

Q: I experience audio delay when mirroring to a TV box.
Try enabling the option to play sound from your phone speaker in Settings. This can help reduce audio latency while casting.

Q: Which TVs are supported?
Any TV or streaming device that supports AirPlay (or an HDMI connection from your iPhone) should work. Performance may vary depending on your network and TV device.
